Professional Corporate Secretarial Services in Narre Warren and Melbourne

Setting up a corporate structure can greatly affect the future of your business. Taking help from professional accountants who can further proof your structures and provide qualified corporate advisory services can save you from a lot of difficulties.

Taxus Hume offers professional corporate secretarial services that include:

  • Corporate Secretarial Services
  • Establishment of companies, trusts, and self-managed superannuation funds (SMSF)
  • Registration of new companies and deregistration of old ones
  • Maintenance of company and trust registers and records
  • Trust deed variations
  • SMSF deed variations and updates
  • Registered office service
  • Business name registrations and renewals
  • Registrations for ABN, TFN, GST
  • Change of company name
  • ASIC compliance
  • Management of ASIC annual returns
  • Preparation of documentation to notify ASIC of changes, including change of name and change of registered address details.
